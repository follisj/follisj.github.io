Where patterns meet possibility—and you lead the discovery.

The Department of Mathematics and Computer Science invites you to explore the language of patterns, logic, and discovery. Here, numbers, algorithms, and ideas are not just tools but gateways to understanding the world—and reshaping it.

In our liberal-arts setting, you’ll pair rigorous thinking with creative making. From elegant proofs to ethical AI, you’ll learn to connect ideas across disciplines and build tools that matter for people and communities.
