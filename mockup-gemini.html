Our Degree Programs

B.A. in Mathematics

Focus on **theoretical foundations**, logic, and advanced problem-solving. Core coursework covers Calculus, Linear Algebra, Abstract Algebra, and Real Analysis. Students may choose tracks in Pure or Applied Mathematics.

  • Core focus on Proof Writing
  • Electives in Topology or Number Theory
  • Excellent preparation for Graduate School
View Math Requirements

B.A. in Computer Science

A rigorous program centered on **algorithm design**, data structures, and the principles of software engineering. Strong emphasis on the mathematical theory underpinning computation.

  • Core focus on Discrete Math & Logic
  • Electives in AI, Graphics, or Systems
  • Portfolio development encouraged
View CS Requirements

Interdisciplinary Minor: Data Science

A collaborative minor combining statistics, computational methods, and ethical reasoning for handling large datasets. Perfect for majors across all liberal arts disciplines.

  • Requires Statistics and Programming courses
  • Focus on Data Visualization
  • Ethical Data Handling Module
